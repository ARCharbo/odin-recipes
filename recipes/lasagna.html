Lasagna

Easy homemade lasagna recipe

Ingredients

Bolognese

  • 2 lb. ground pork
  • 1 lb. ground beef chuck (20% fat)
  • Kosher salt, freshly ground black pepper
  • 2 tbsp. of extra-virgin olive oil
  • 2 oz. pancetta
  • 1 celery stalk, finely chopped
  • 1 medium carrot, peeled, finely chopped
  • 6 garlic cloves, sliced
  • 2 tbsp. tomato paste
  • 3/4 cup dry white wine
  • 28-oz. can whole peeled tomato
  • 1 cup low-sodium chicken broth
  • 1 cup of whole milk

Bechamel

  • 7 tbsp. unsalted butter
  • 1/4 cup plus 3 tbdp. all-purpose flour
  • Kosher salt, freshly ground black pepper
  • 2 tbsp. of extra-virgin olive oil
  • 4 oz. parmesan cheese, coarsely grated (about 1 cup)
  • 6 cups of whole milk
  • pinch of cayenne pepper
  • pinch of ground nutmeg
  • 1 1/4 lb. dried lasagna noodles
  • extra-virgin olive oil

Steps

Bolognese

  1. Preheat oven to 225°
  2. Mix the meat in a large bowl & season with salt and pepper
  3. Form large meatballs
  4. Warm 2 tbsp. of extra-virgin olive oil
  5. Cook meatballs
  6. Add pancetta, onion, celery, carrot & garlic
  7. Add tomato paste
  8. Add white wine
  9. Add low-sodium chicken broth & whole milk
  10. extra-virgin olive oil

Bechamel

  1. Melt butter
  2. Whisk in flour and then whole milk
  3. Form large meatballs
  4. Remove from heat and whisk in parmesan
  5. Add cayenne pepper and ground nutmeg, then salt and pepper
  6. Preheat oven to 235°
  7. Cook the lasagna noodles
